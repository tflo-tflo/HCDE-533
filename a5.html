I gravitated to either geometric forms or playful organic "bulbous" ones. Chispa's and, I think Nemo's, had dynamic parts to control the direction of the light. I like how Hay's structure supported it to stand upright without explicit feet.

Lamp planning

April 27th, 2024

I took apart one of my table lamps to see how they constructed theirs. It was also helpfuul to help me think through the parts I'll need.

I consulted with Nathan on the direction and feasibility of my lamp ideas. Considerations we discussed:

  1. Lightbulb heat - Possible effects on melting the PLA
  2. Weight - The lamp innerds and cord will naturally shift the weight of a 3D print
  3. Light strength - The brightness will depend on the bulb, the number of walls for the shade, and of the course the design for the light direction
