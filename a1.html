Asssignment 1
Modelling and laser cutting: 30 instances of one unique part

Make a kit consisting of at least 30 instances of at least one unique part! The parts must have slots that press fit into the other parts in the kit! The parts must be laser cut out of cardboard! Your fit should be such that the parts do not come apart with a light shaking!

Ideation

March 30th, 2024

With a little inspiration from Kenya Hara's "Architecture for Dogs" I decided to make something that could be used by my friend's cats.

After sketching a few options, I consulted a friend who's done laser cut models on the scope and difficulty of each idea. We agreed idea B seemed doable while still a challenge. I could manually trim some of the slats down to size and cut out a quarter of the bottom circular supports if I wanted to make option C.
